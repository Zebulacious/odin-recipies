HOW TO MAKE OATMEAL

You will not want to eat this. It honestly does not taste that good. However, it has one major benefit. The oatmeal is chalk full of slow releasing carbs. When I say slow releasing carbs, I mean the the oatmeal is not composed of the ever so prevelant refined carbs you see today!

You will nead...

  • a bowl
  • water
  • oatmeal packet
  • microwave

Steps

  1. fill the bowl with the dry oatmeal
  2. fill the empty oatmeal packet up with water until the brown line
  3. pour the water in the bowl
  4. heat for 1 min. stir. heat for 30 more seconds.
